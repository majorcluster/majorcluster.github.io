About Us

Our Company

Major Cluster was founded by Mateus Carvalho in 2023 with a mission to provide innovative and customer-tailored solutions that empower businesses to thrive along with empowering the open source community with robust and well crafted contributions. With a team of skilled and experienced professionals, we specialize in cutting-edge technologies, including software development, cloud computing, and cybersecurity, among others.

At our tech company, we believe that technology should be accessible to everyone. That's why we are committed to contributing to Open Source initiatives, which allow businesses to access high-quality, open-source software solutions at no cost. We also prioritize customer-tailored solutions, working closely with our clients to develop solutions that meet their unique business needs and objectives.

Our dedication to providing innovative, customer-tailored solutions and contributing to Free Software initiatives has enabled us to establish a strong reputation as a trusted partner for businesses looking to stay ahead of the curve.
